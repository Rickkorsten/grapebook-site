Common questions

How do I restore my Pro subscription?

Go to Account → Membership → Restore purchases. Subscriptions are tied to your Apple ID, so they follow you to a new device.

How do I cancel Grapebook Pro?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple: open Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone and select Grapebook.

I used the app as a guest — are my notes safe?

Guest notes live on your device only. Create a free account from the app and your existing notes are kept and synced.

How do I delete my account?

In the app: Account → Delete account. This permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone.

Is there an Android version?

It's on the way. The app is built to run on Android — we're finishing store setup and testing.

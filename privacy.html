Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026 · Controller: Rick Korsten — rickkorsten@live.nl

This Privacy Policy explains how Grapebook ("the app", "we", "us") collects, uses and shares your information when you use the Grapebook mobile application. By using the app you agree to this policy. The short version: your journal is yours, we don't sell data, and there is no advertising and no third-party tracking.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you create an account we collect your email address, your password (stored securely, hashed, by our authentication provider), and optionally your first name, last name and username (@handle). You can also use the app as an anonymous guest, in which case no email is required until you choose to create an account.
  • Content you create. We store the tasting notes you add (wine details, colour, aromas, score, text, price, occasion, etc.) and any photos you attach (e.g. wine labels).
  • AI label scan. If you use the optional AI label scan, the photo you capture is sent to Google (Gemini API) to read the label and suggest wine details. We do not use these images to train models; they are processed only to return the result to you.
  • Friends & sharing. If you use the social features, we store your friend requests, friendships, shared notes, comments and wishlist. Your name and @handle are visible to other signed-in users so they can find and connect with you. A pour you share is visible to the specific friend(s) you send it to.
  • Notifications & language. If you enable push notifications, we store a device push token and your notification preferences. We also store your app language so notifications arrive in your language.
  • Purchases. Subscriptions are processed by Apple and managed via RevenueCat. We (through RevenueCat) receive your subscription status and a pseudonymous app user identifier. We do not receive or store your payment card details.
  • Device & local data. If you enable biometric sign-in (Face ID / Touch ID), the related credential is stored only on your device (in the secure keychain) and is never sent to us.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and operate the app (your account, notes, photos and social features).
  • To process the AI label scan you request.
  • To send the notifications you opted in to (e.g. friend requests, badges).
  • To process and restore your subscription.
  • To keep the service secure and to debug and improve it.
  • To communicate with you about your account or important changes.

3. Who we share it with (sub-processors)

  • Supabase — database, authentication and file storage (hosting region: European Union).
  • Google (Gemini API) — only the images you submit to the AI label scan.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • Apple — App Store payments, subscription billing and push notification delivery.
  • Expo — push notification delivery service.

Each processes data only to provide their service to us. We may also disclose information if required by law.

4. Where your data is stored

Your account data, notes and photos are stored with Supabase on servers in the European Union. Some sub-processors (e.g. Google, RevenueCat, Apple, Expo) may process limited data outside the EU under appropriate safeguards.

5. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymise your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain certain records (e.g. for legal or accounting reasons). Guest (anonymous) data stays on your device until you create an account or remove the app.

6. Your rights & deleting your account

You can delete your account directly in the app: Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, tasting notes, photos, friends and shares, and cannot be undone.

Depending on your location (e.g. under the EU GDPR) you also have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at rickkorsten@live.nl. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in your Apple App Store account settings.

7. Children

Grapebook is intended for users who are of legal drinking age in their country and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, hashed passwords, access controls via our providers) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email rickkorsten@live.nl.
